Peanut Butter Chocolate Pretzel Pie

This peanut butter chocolate pretzel pie has a lush cream cheese and peanut butter layer atop a lightly baked pretzel crust, finished layers of chocolate pudding, whipped topping, and crushed chocolate peanut butter candy.

Ingredients

  • 2 1/4 cups crushed pretzels
  • 1/4 cup white sugar
  • 3/4 cup butter, melted
  • 2 (8 ounce) packages cream cheese, softened
  • 1 cup creamy peanut butter
  • 1 1/2 cups confectioner's sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 16 ounces whipped topping
  • 1 (5.9 ounce) package instant chocolate pudding
  • 2 1/2 cups cold milk
  • 1/2 cup chopped peanut butter cups and coated peanut butter candies, such as Reese's PiecesÂ®, or as needed for topping

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F(180 degrees C). Stir pretzels, sugar and melted butter together and press lightly into a 9x13-inch baking dish.
  2. Bake in the preheated oven just until fragrant, about 10 minutes. Let cool to room temperature, about 20 minutes.
  3. Beat cream cheese, peanut butter, confectioner's sugar and vanilla together with an electric mixer until well blended, light and fluffy. Fold in 2 1/2 cups whipped topping.
  4. Spread peanut butter mixture evenly over cooled crust.
  5. Whisk together chocolate pudding mix and milk until smooth, well combined and slightly thickened.
  6. Carefully spread chocolate pudding mixture over peanut butter layer, taking care not to mix layers together. Top with remaining whipped topping.
  7. Sprinkle with chopped candies and refrigerate for at least 4 hours.
